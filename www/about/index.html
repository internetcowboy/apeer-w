Our Team

Christopher Borhani

Co-Founder & CEO

Connecting talented people is what Christopher does best. Before launching Apeer to advance the legal practice, Christopher joined forces with a colleague from law school to build a firm from the ground up in San Francisco. There, he gained exceptional appearance results by empowering the attorneys that produce them.

Christopher previously held positions in film production at Focus Features [Universal Pictures], legal affairs at Summit Entertainment [Lionsgate], and several law firms across Los Angeles. An entrepreneur by nature, he enjoys finding ways to improve the quality of life. His wolfdog loftmates care less about apps but do look forward to his occasional three-in-the-morning piano concerts.

Codin Pangell

Co-Founder & CTO

Conceiving simple solutions to complex problems and elegantly executing his vision has earned Codin the opportunity to work with many high-profile clients including Nike, Microsoft, Hulu, Disney, Lionsgate, Fox and Dodge. Drawing on a decade of experience programming and implementing mobile solutions, digital installations, APIs, and databases, Codin sparks technical ingenuity to life at Apeer with innovative features and capabilities.

His exceptional toolkit saves Apeer members valuable time and resources while giving them the opportunity to grow and elevate their practice.

Josh Pangell

Co-Founder

It takes more than hard work to turn a concept into a pioneering organization fueled by passionate members. Having accomplished this more than once, Josh has the unique knowledge and ability to make Apeer a remarkable service.

As a co-founder at Cargo, he developed a social platform adopted by hundreds of thousands of artists to share work, ideas, and inspiration. Before that, he engineered another global network that enables scientists, technologists, and futurists to collaborate on cutting-edge projects. Until he can explore space for real, Josh pilots recreational aircraft for fun.
